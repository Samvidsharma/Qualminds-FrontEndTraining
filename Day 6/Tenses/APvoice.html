Assertive/Declarative Sentence
Tense Present Tense Past Tense Future Tense
Simple Tense I eat a mango. I ate a mango. I shall eat a mango.
A mango is eaten by me. A mango was eaten by me. A mango will be eaten by me.
Continous Tense I am eating a mango. I was eating a mango. I shall be eating a mango.
A mango is being eaten by me. A mango was being eaten by me. No Passive voice.
Perferct Tense I have eaten a mango. I had eaten a mango. I shall have eaten a mango.
A mango has been eaten by me. A mango had been eaten by me. A mango shall has been eaten by me.
Perfect Continous Tense I have been eating a mango. I had been eating a mango. I shall have been eating a mango.
